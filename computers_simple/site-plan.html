Overview

Purpose

My name is Juan Zurita, I’m a student at BYU Idaho and I am learning Web Development. Have you ever needed to buy a computer or laptop and can’t really decide which one to choose? Don’t you know that much about PCs and are confused between all those tech-related confusing words? This web page will explain you, without any background knowledge requirement, which components are better and why; it will also recommend the best laptops, pc builds and upgrades in the market now. Everything in a very simple and easy way.

Audience

This page is riented to people who want to learn more about computers and have no background of it.
